Pokhara

From Pokhara, you can see three out of the ten highest mountains in the world (Dhaulagiri, Annapurna, Manasalu). The Machhapuchre (“Fishtail”) has become the icon of the city, thanks to its pointed peak. As the base for trekkers who make the popular Annapurna Circuit, Pokhara offers many sightseeing opportunities.
